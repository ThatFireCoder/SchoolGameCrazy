# SchoolGameCrazy - Flappy Bird Game 🐦

A fun and addictive Flappy Bird game built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Play it directly in your browser!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Start the Game**: Click the "START GAME" button or press SPACE
2. **Make the Bird Fly**: 
   - Click anywhere on the game canvas, OR
   - Press the SPACE bar
3. **Avoid the Pipes**: Navigate through the gaps in the pipes
4. **Score Points**: Earn 1 point for each pipe you successfully pass
5. **Track Your Best**: Your highest score is saved automatically

## 🕹️ Controls

- **Mouse Click** or **SPACE Bar** - Make the bird jump/flap

## 🎯 Game Mechanics

- **Gravity**: The bird continuously falls and accelerates
- **Flapping**: Pressing space/clicking gives the bird an upward velocity
- **Pipes**: Randomly spaced pipes appear and move from right to left
- **Scoring**: Pass through a pipe gap to earn a point
- **Game Over**: Hitting a pipe or the ground ends the game
- **Best Score**: Automatically saved using browser's local storage
